Privacy Policy & Terms

Effective date: June 2026  |  gobelles.com

1. About This Policy

Texas Belles Fastpitch ("Texas Belles," "we," "us") operates the tryout registration platform at gobelles.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through our website, including phone numbers provided for SMS communication.

2. Information We Collect

When a parent or guardian submits a tryout registration form at gobelles.com, we collect: parent name, player name, phone number, email address, player date of birth, and responses to our parent fit questionnaire. This information is used solely to facilitate communication regarding Texas Belles tryouts and organizational updates.

3. SMS Messaging

By submitting the tryout registration form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Texas Belles Fastpitch coaching staff regarding tryout scheduling, roster decisions, event reminders, and organization updates. Message frequency varies based on tryout activity, typically 2–10 messages per tryout cycle. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive from us. To request help, reply HELP.

4. No Sharing of Mobile Numbers

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your mobile phone number to any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers collected through gobelles.com are used exclusively for direct communication between Texas Belles coaching staff and the parent or guardian who provided the number.

5. Data Storage

Registration data is stored securely using Supabase, a SOC 2 compliant cloud database provider. Access is restricted to authorized Texas Belles coaching staff only.

6. Children's Privacy

Our registration form is completed by parents and guardians on behalf of minor players. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from minors under the age of 13.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy or to request removal of your data, contact us at: info@gobelles.com

Terms & Conditions

By submitting the tryout registration form at gobelles.com you agree to the following:

Tryout participation does not guarantee roster placement. Texas Belles coaching staff retain full discretion over all roster decisions.

SMS consent is provided voluntarily. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Opting out does not affect your player's tryout status.

Accuracy of information: You represent that all information submitted in the registration form is accurate and that you are the parent or legal guardian of the player listed.

Communication: Texas Belles may contact you via SMS or email using the contact information provided in the registration form for purposes directly related to tryouts and team participation.

Texas Belles Fastpitch reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of gobelles.com constitutes acceptance of any updates.
